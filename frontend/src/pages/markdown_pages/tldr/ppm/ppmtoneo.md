# ppmtoneo

> Convert a PPM image to an Atari Neochrome file.
> More information: <https://netpbm.sourceforge.net/doc/ppmtoneo.html>.

- Convert a PPM image to a NEO file:

`ppmtoneo {{path/to/file.ppm}} > {{path/to/file.neo}}`
