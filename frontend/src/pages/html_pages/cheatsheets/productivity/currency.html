How to Use the Currency Converter

Using the currency converter is straightforward. You can interact with it in two primary ways:

Guided Conversion

Simply type currency in your terminal to initiate an interactive session. The tool will then guide you through the process of selecting base and target currencies and entering the amount for conversion.

Direct Conversion with Arguments

For quick conversions, you can supply the base currency, the target currency, and the amount directly as arguments to the currency command. The format is as follows: 

currency [Base Currency] [Target Currency] [Amount]

For example, to convert 14.38 USD to EUR, you would use:

currency USD EUR 14.38
