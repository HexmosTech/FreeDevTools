NAME
       signal-rr-recording - send a signal to processes being recorded by rr


SYNOPSIS
       signal-rr-recording <signal>

       sends signal, as understood by kill(1), to all processes being recorded by rr(1).


SEE ALSO
       rr(1), kill(1), signal(7)
