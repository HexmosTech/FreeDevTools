NAME
       showtime - Watch without distraction


SYNOPSIS
       showtime [OPTION…] [VIDEO FILES]


DESCRIPTION
       Play your favorite movies and video files without hassle. Showtime features simple playback controls that
       fade  out  of your way when you're watching, fullscreen, adjustable playback speed, multiple language and
       subtitle tracks, and screenshots — everything you need for a straightforward viewing experience.

       You can set showtime as the default video player in GNOME or just launch the app and open the video file.


OPTIONS
       -h, --help - Show help options

       --help-all - Show all help options

       --help-gapplication - Show GApplication options

       --gapplication-service - Enter GApplication service mode (use from D-Bus service files)

       -n, --new-window           Open the app with a new window
