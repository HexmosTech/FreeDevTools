NAME
       tintii - Turns colour photos into b/w and highlights regions in colour


SYNOPSIS
       tintii [-h --verbose]


DESCRIPTION
       tintii  takes  full  colour  photos  and  processes  them  into  black and white with some select regions
       highlighted in colour. The technique is known as colour popping or selective colouring - tintii makes  it
       easy.
