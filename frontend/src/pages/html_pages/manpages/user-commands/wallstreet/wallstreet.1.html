NAME
       wallstreet - fill your console with wallstreet melodrama technobabble


SYNOPSIS
       wallstreet [SPLITS]


OPTIONS
           SPLITS  The number of splits to divide your screen


DESCRIPTION
       This utility will fill your console with Wall Street technobabble.

       It  will split your byobu(1) console into a multiple panes of news and statistics, like any good computer
       screen on Wall Street.

       To quit, press Ctrl-C to kill the wallstreet script itself.


SEE ALSO
       byobu(1), nice(1)
