NAME
       wiipdf - present using xpdf and your wiimote


SYNOPSIS
       wiipdf bluetooth-id pdf-file


DESCRIPTION
       wiipdf  is  a  tiny  tool  to present a PDF using xpdf and your wiimote. You start it by providing it the
       bluetooth ID of your wiimote (use hcitool scan to get it) and the path to the PDF you want to present.

       wiipdf then tries to connect to your wiimote (press buttons 1 and 2 at the sime time on your  wiimote  to
       enter  discoverable  mode).  As soon as the connection is established (takes 3-4 seconds usually), wiipdf
       launches xpdf with the given filename.

       You can now press A or B on your wiimote to go forward one slide or go backward one slide.  Pressing  the
       home  button  on your wiimote ends the presentation. Each keypress is confirmed by a short rumble of your
       wiimote.


BUGS
       wiipdf does not know how many slides your PDF consists of. Therefore, you can press A as  many  times  as
       you want, even if you are at the end of the PDF. The internal slide counter will just be increased.
