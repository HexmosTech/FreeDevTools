NAME
       bundle-show - Shows all the gems in your bundle, or the path to a gem


SYNOPSIS
       bundle show [GEM] [--paths] [--outdated]


DESCRIPTION
       Without  the [GEM] option, show will print a list of the names and versions of all gems that are required
       by your [Gemfile(5)][Gemfile(5)], sorted by name.

       Calling show with [GEM] will list the exact location of that gem on your machine.


OPTIONS
       --paths
              List the paths of all gems that are required by your [Gemfile(5)][Gemfile(5)], sorted by gem name.

       --outdated
              Show verbose output including whether gems are outdated.
