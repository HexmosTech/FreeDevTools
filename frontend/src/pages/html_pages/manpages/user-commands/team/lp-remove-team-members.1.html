NAME
       lp-remove-team-members - remove members from a Launchpad team


SYNOPSIS
       lp-remove-team-members <team> <MEMBER>...


DESCRIPTION
       lp-remove-team-members allows you to quickly bulk remove users from a team.


OPTIONS
       Listed below are the command line options for lp-remove-team-members:

       <team> Launchpad team to remove members from

       <MEMBER>...
              List of members to remove from the team
