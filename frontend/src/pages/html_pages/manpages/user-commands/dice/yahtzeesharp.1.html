NAME
       yahtzeesharp - sort of poker with dice and less money


DESCRIPTION
       Yahtzeesharp  is  an  implementation of yahtzee.  Yahtzee is sort of poker with dice and less money.  You
       roll five dice three times and try to create the best "hand."  Your two rerolls may include any or all of
       your dice.
