NAME
       bleak - Bluetooth Low Energy platform agnostic client


SYNOPSIS
       bleak [-h] [-i ADAPTER] [-t TIMEOUT]


DESCRIPTION
       Perform Bluetooth Low Energy device scan


OPTIONS
       -i ADAPTER
              HCI device

       -t TIMEOUT
              Duration to scan for
