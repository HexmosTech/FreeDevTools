   Strftime Patterns
       %c (%a %d %b %Y %T) - date time format

          2008-02-05T18:30:30 = ma 05 Feb 2008 18:30:30
          1995-12-22T09:05:02 = ve 22 Dec 1995 09:05:02
         -0010-09-15T04:44:23 = sa 15 Sep -10 04:44:23

       %x (%Y-%m-%d) - date format
