   Strftime Patterns
       %c (%a %b %e %H:%M:%S %Y) - date time format

          2008-02-05T18:30:30 = Sib Nhlo  5 18:30:30 2008
          1995-12-22T09:05:02 = Sih Mpal 22 09:05:02 1995
         -0010-09-15T04:44:23 = Mgq Mpan 15 04:44:23 -10

          1995-12-22T09:05:02 = 12/22/95
