   Strftime Patterns
       %c (Te %A, te %d o %B, %Y %T) - date time format

          2008-02-05T18:30:30 = Te Tūrei, te 05 o Pēpuere, 2008 18:30:30
          1995-12-22T09:05:02 = Te Paraire, te 22 o Tīhema, 1995 09:05:02
         -0010-09-15T04:44:23 = Te Rāhoroi, te 15 o Hepetema, -10 04:44:23

       %x (%d/%m/%y) - date format

          2008-02-05T18:30:30 = 05/02/08
          1995-12-22T09:05:02 = 22/12/95
         -0010-09-15T04:44:23 = 15/09/10

       %X (%T) - time format

          2008-02-05T18:30:30 = 18:30:30
          1995-12-22T09:05:02 = 09:05:02
         -0010-09-15T04:44:23 = 04:44:23
