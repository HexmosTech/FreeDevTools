Macro Definition Documentation
   #define GLOBUS_GSI_CALLBACK_VERIFY_DEPTH   100
       Constant passed to SSL_CTX_set_verify_depth() and SSL_set_verify_depth() to increase allowed certificate
       depth to 100 rather than the OpenSSL default of 9. 100 is an arbitrarily chosen large value.
