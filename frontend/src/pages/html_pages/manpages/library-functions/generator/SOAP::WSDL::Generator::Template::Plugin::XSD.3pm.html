NAME
       SOAP::WSDL::Generator::Template::Plugin::XSD - Template plugin for the XSD generator


METHODS
   perl_name
        XSD.perl_name(element.get_name);

       Converts a XML name into a valid perl name (valid for subroutines, variables or the like).

       perl_name takes a crude approach by just replacing . and - (dot and dash) with a underscore. This may or
       may not be sufficient, and may or may not provoke collisions in your XML names.
