           "Color: Universal Language and Dictionary of Names",
           National Bureau of Standards,
           Spec. Publ. 440, Dec. 1976, 189 pages.

           Color Name Dictionary: (RC) Rock-Color Chart

           National Research Council,
           Rock-Color Chart

       This combinatorial field-work dictionary's 95 names map to 120 colors, using 89 of 267 centoids. This is
       less redundant than the others. Unfortunately, the focus on rock colors will not make for a general
       purpose dictionary.

       <http://swissnet.ai.mit.edu/~jaffer/Color/Dictionaries#RC>
