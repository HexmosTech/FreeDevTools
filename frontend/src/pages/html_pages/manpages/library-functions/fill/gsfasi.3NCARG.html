NAME
       GSFASI (Set fill are style index) - sets the fill area style index.


SYNOPSIS
       CALL GSFASI (STYLI)


C-BINDING SYNOPSIS
       #include <ncarg/gks.h>

       void gset_fill_style_ind(Gint fill_style_ind);


DESCRIPTION
       STYLI       (Integer,  Input)  -  Specifies  workstation-dependent  options  for  hatch or pattern fills.
                   Pattern fill options are not supported in NCAR Graphics. Hatch fill options are:

                   1      Horizontal lines (default)

                   2      Vertical lines

                   3      Lines of positive slope

                   4      Lines of negative slope

                   5      Horizontal and vertical lines

                   6      Lines of positive and negative slope


ACCESS
       To use GKS routines, load the NCAR GKS-0A library ncarg_gks.
