   QVector< QwtInterval > QwtClipper::clipCircle (const QRectF & clipRect, const QPointF & center, double
       radius) [static]
       Circle clipping

       clipCircle() divides a circle into intervals of angles representing arcs of the circle. When the circle
       is completely inside the clip rectangle an interval [0.0, 2 * M_PI] is returned.

       Parameters:
           clipRect Clip rectangle
           center Center of the circle
           radius Radius of the circle

       Returns:
           Arcs of the circle
