       • math  - Mathematical

       •   $M_E - Euler's Constant e

       •   $M_LOG2E - Log base 2 of e

       •   $M_LOG10E  - Log base 10 of e

       •   $M_SQRT2  - Square root of 2

       •   $M_SQRT1_2  - Square root of 1/2

       •   $M_SQRT3  - Square root of 3

       •   $M_PI - Pi

       •   $M_PI_2 - Pi divided by 2

       •   $M_PI_4  - Pi divided by 4

       •   $M_SQRTPI  - Square root of pi

       •   $M_2_SQRTPI - Two over the square root of pi

       •   $M_1_PI  - One over pi

       •   $M_2_PI  - Two over pi

       •   $M_LN10  - Natural log of 10

       •   $M_LN2   - Natural log of 2

       •   $M_LNPI  - Natural log of pi
