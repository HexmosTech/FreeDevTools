NAME
       yaz - Z39.50 toolkit.


DESCRIPTION
       YAZ is a C/C++ programmer's toolkit supporting the development of Z39.50v3 clients and servers. The YAZ
       toolkit offers several different levels of access to the ISO23950/Z39.50, SRU Solr (client only) and ILL
       protocols. The level that you need to use depends on your requirements, and the role (server or client)
       that you want to implement.
