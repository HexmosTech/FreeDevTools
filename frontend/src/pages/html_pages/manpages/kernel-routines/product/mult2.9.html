NAME
       mult2 - Product of two inputs


SYNOPSIS

       loadrt mult2 [count=N|names=name1[,name2...]]


FUNCTIONS
       mult2.N (requires a floating-point thread)


PINS
       mult2.N.in0 float in
       mult2.N.in1 float in
       mult2.N.out float out
              out = in0 * in1


SEE ALSO
        invert(9), div2(9)
