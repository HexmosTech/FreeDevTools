NAME
       enum nvme_unit - Defined buffer size and write throughput granularity units


SYNOPSIS
       enum nvme_unit {
           NVME_UNIT_B ,
           NVME_UNIT_1K ,
           NVME_UNIT_1M ,
           NVME_UNIT_1G

       };


Constants
       NVME_UNIT_B Bytes or Bytes/second

       NVME_UNIT_1K
                   1 KiB or 1 KiB/second

       NVME_UNIT_1M
                   1 MiB or 1 MiB/second

       NVME_UNIT_1G
                   1 GiB or 1 GiB/second
