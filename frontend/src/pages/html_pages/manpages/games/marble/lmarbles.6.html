NAME
       lmarbles - build figures out of colored marbles


SYNOPSIS
       lmarbles [--version]


DESCRIPTION
       Your  goal  in  the puzzle game lmarbles is to create a more or less complex figure out of single marbles
       within a time limit to reach the next level. Sounds easy? Well, there is a problem: If a marble starts to
       move, it will not stop until it hits a wall or another marble.


OPTIONS
       --version Show version information and exit.


FILES
       ~/.lgames/lmarbles.conf
              Automatically generated configfile. This is not meant for human editing.

       /var/games/lmarbles/lmarbles.prfs
              Global preferences and score file. Not for human editing.
