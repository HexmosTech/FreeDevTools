NAME
       LightsOff - Turn off all the lights


SYNOPSIS
       lightsoff [OPTION...]


DESCRIPTION
       Lights  Off is a collection of light switch puzzles. Clicking on a light toggles it either on or off, but
       also toggles all the surrounding lights, too. Your objective is simple: turn off all the lights!

       Players who delight in turning off lights will enjoy the huge number of levels.


OPTIONS
       -v, --version
              Prints the program version and exits.

       This program also accepts the standard GTK+ options.
