NAME
       kaces - X window aces high solitaire


SYNOPSIS
       kaces


DESCRIPTION
       Kaces  brings  up  a  window for a aces solatiaire game.  Clicking on a card moves it to the "best" legal
       play. Dragging a card places it (if legal) in the target stack.


RULES
       Top cards of tableaus are available, and may be discarded if they have the same suit and lower rank  than
       another  available  card.   Empty  tableaus  may be filled with any available card. Aces rank high, above
       Kings.

       Deal four more cards by clickping the deck. Dealing is not allowed until empty tableaus have been  filled
       (if possible).

       Goal: discard all cards except for the four Aces.
