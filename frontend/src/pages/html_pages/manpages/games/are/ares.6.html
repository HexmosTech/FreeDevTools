NAME
       ares - Accurate multi-system emulator


SYNOPSIS
       ares


DESCRIPTION
       ares is an emulator for systems from Atari, Nintendo, Sega, Sony, NEC, SNK, Microsoft, Coleco, Bandai and
       Benesse.  It  is  a  descendent  of  higan  and  bsnes and focuses on accuracy and preservation. The SNES
       emulation is especially complete and polished.


MORE INFO
       Website: https://ares-emulator.github.io/
