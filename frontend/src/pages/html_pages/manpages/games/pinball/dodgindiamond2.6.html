NAME
       dodgindiamond2 - little arcade game shooter for one or two players


SYNOPSIS
       dodgindiamond2


DESCRIPTION
       This manual page documents briefly the dodgindiamond2 command.

       dodgindiamond2  aims  to  be  an  old school arcade game with low resolution graphics, top-down scrolling
       action, energy based gameplay, and different weapons with several levels of power.


USAGE
       Player 1 (Red)
              Left - Left
              Right - Right
              Up - Forward
              Down - Back
              m - Fire

       Player 2 (Blue)
              Left - Left
              Right - Right
              Up - Forward
              Down - Back
              Ctrl - Fire
