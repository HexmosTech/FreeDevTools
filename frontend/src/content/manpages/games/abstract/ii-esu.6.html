NAME
       ii-esu - abstract shooter


DESCRIPTION
       Your  ship is surrounded by two circles. You shoot in the direction of your mouse points, and you move if
       the mouse pointer is outside the inner circle.

       You can find more about the game on its homepage: http://hizuoka.web.fc2.com/game/ES/index_en.html
