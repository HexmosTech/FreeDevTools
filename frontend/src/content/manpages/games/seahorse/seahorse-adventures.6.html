NAME
       Seahorse-Adventures - help Barbie the seahorse float on bubbles to the moon


SYNOPSIS
       seahorse-adventures [OPTION...]


DESCRIPTION
       Barbie Seahorse Adventures is a retro style platform arcade game in the spirit of Mario 3. You are Barbie
       the seahorse who travels through the jungle, up to the volcano until you float on bubbles to the moon.

       On  the  way  to your final destination you will encounter various enemies, servants of the evil overlord
       who stole the galaxy crystal. Avoid getting hit and defeat them with your bubbles!

       Use your arrow keys or your joystick to move the seahorse. You can open doors by pressing the UP or  DOWN
       key. Enemies take 3 shots unless you are powered up!

       ALT or x
              Fire

       CTRL or Space or z
              Jump

       F10    GOD MODE


OPTIONS
       -full
              run in fullscreen mode.

       -lowres
              run the game in 320x240 resolution.

       -scale2x
              use the scale2x scaler.

       -tv
              produce a scanline effect.
