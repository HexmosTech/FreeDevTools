NAME
       filler - simple game where two players try to capture half the board


SYNOPSIS
       filler


DESCRIPTION
       This manual page documents briefly the filler game by John Farrell.
       Filler is a simple game where two players try to capture half of the board.  One player starts at the hex
       marked  L  and  the  other starts at the hex marked R.  Each player takes turns choosing a colour. When a
       player chooses a colour, all board hexes owned by that player change to that colour.  The  colour  change
       captures  all adjacent hexes which match the player's new colour.  The game keeps score of how many hexes
       each player has captured.
       The winner is the first player to control more than half the hexes. As there are 1377 hexes, you  need  a
       score of 689 to win.
