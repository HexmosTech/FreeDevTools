NAME
       fortune-zh - Chinese Data files for fortune


SYNOPSIS
       fortune-zh


DESCRIPTION
       fortune-zh  is  a  simple  shell  script  that  invokes  fortune with Chinese data files and their preset
       proportions.


DATAFILE
       /usr/share/games/fortunes/tang300
              About 300 Poetries from Tang dynasty.

       /usr/share/games/fortunes/song100
              About 100 Poetries from Song dynasty.

       /usr/share/games/fortunes/chinese
              Miscellaneous Cookies in Chinese language, both ancient and modern, excluding  those  tang300  and
              song100 already included.
