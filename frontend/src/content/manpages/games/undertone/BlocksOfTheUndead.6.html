NAME
       BlocksOfTheUndead - Tetris Attack clone with spooky undertones


SYNOPSIS
       BlocksOfTheUndead


DESCRIPTION
       The aim of the game is to remove all the blocks from the field.
       Blocks  are  removed  when you place three or more of them in a row by swapping them. Move the cursor and
       swap the two blocks under the cursor by clicking on them.
