NAME
       triangle - reference element (rheolef-7.2)


DESCRIPTION
       The triangle reference_element(6) is K = [0,1].

           K = { 0 < x0 < 1 and 0 < x1 < 1-x0 }

           x1
           2
           | +
           |   +
           |     +
           |       +
           0---------1  x0

        This two-dimensional reference_element(6) is then transformed, after the Piola geometrical application,
       as a triangle in a 2D or 3D physical space, as a geo_element(6).

       Curved high order transformed geo_element(6) Pk triangle (k >= 1) are supported for 2D or 3D geometries.
       In these cases, the nodes of an high-order triangle are numbered as:

       Note that high-order triangles have additional edge-nodes and face-nodes. These nodes are numbered as:
       first vertices, then edge-nodes, following the edge numbering order and orientation, and finally the face
       internal nodes, following the triangle lattice.

         2               2               2
         | +             | +             | +
         |   +           7   6           9   8
         5     4         |     +        10 14  7
         |       +       8   9   5      11 12 13 6
         |         +     |         +     |         +
         0-----3-----1   0---3---4---1   0--3--4--5--1
               P2              P3              P4
