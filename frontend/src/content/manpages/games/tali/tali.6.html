NAME
       Tali - A variation on poker with dice and less money.


SYNOPSIS
       tali [OPTION...]


DESCRIPTION
       GNOME Tali is a sort of poker with dice and less money.  You roll five dice three times and try to create
       the best hand. Your two rerolls may include any or all of your dice.


OPTIONS
       -d, --delay
              Delay computer moves

       -t, --thoughts
              Display computer thoughts

       -n, --computers=NUMBER
              Number of computer opponents

       -p, --humans=NUMBER
              Number of human opponents

       -g, --game=STRING
              Game choice: Regular or Colours

       This program also accepts the standard GNOME and GTK options.
