NAME
       Four-in-a-Row - four-in-a-row game for GNOME


SYNOPSIS
       four-in-a-row [OPTION...]


DESCRIPTION
       The  object  of  the  game  is to build a line of four of your marbles while trying to stop your opponent
       (human or computer) building a line of his or her own. A line can be horizontal, vertical or diagonal.


OPTIONS
       This program only accepts the standard GNOME and GTK options.
