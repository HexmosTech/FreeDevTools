WeeChat Commands

WeeChat is a highly customizable and extensible chat client for Linux, designed for efficiency and power users. Mastering its commands and shortcuts can significantly enhance your productivity and user experience. This guide provides essential WeeChat commands and shortcuts to help you navigate and manage your chat sessions effectively.

WeeChat Window and Buffer Management

Efficiently switch between different windows and buffers to keep track of conversations and channels.

# To switch to the left buffer:
Ctrl-p, Alt-left

# To switch to the right buffer:
Ctrl-n, Alt-right

# To switch to the next buffer with activity:
Alt-a

# To switch buffers using numerical shortcuts:
Alt-0...9

WeeChat Navigation and Scrolling

Navigate through your chat history and interface elements with ease.

# To scroll buffer title:
F9/F10

# To scroll nick list:
F11/F12

WeeChat Markers and Actions

Utilize markers to keep track of unread messages and perform quick actions.

# To set an unread marker on all windows:
Ctrl-s Ctrl-u
