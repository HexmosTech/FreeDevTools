# ppmtoleaf

> Convert a PPM image to the Interleaf image format.
> More information: <https://netpbm.sourceforge.net/doc/ppmtoleaf.html>.

- Convert a PPM image to a LEAF file:

`ppmtoleaf {{path/to/file.ppm}} > {{path/to/file.leaf}}`
